Our Story

Pioneering the
Spatial Web

Founded in 2020 by a team of ex-gaming engine developers and computer vision researchers, AR Studio was built on a simple premise: the physical and digital worlds should seamlessly co-exist. Today, we build the infrastructure that makes this possible for global enterprises.

Redefining Reality

We believe that augmented reality is the next major computing platform. Our mission is to help companies transition from flat screens to immersive environments.

User-Centric Design

We prioritize natural, intuitive interactions over complex gestures.

Enterprise Security

Our spatial data clouds are built with military-grade encryption.

The Vision

"In 10 years, looking at a flat screen will feel as archaic as looking at a telegram. We are building the foundational layer for a world where digital context is universally accessible."

Our Mission
